Privacy Policy

Effective from 3 July 2026

1. Data controller

Gergő Lódri (private individual) — email: gergolodri6@gmail.com. This website is a personal portfolio and does not carry out any commercial activity.

2. What data is processed and why?

There is only one place on this site where you provide personal data: the contact form. Your name, email address and message are sent to me solely so that I can reply to your enquiry. The legal basis is your consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR), given by voluntarily sending the message.

3. How long is the data kept?

Messages are kept until our correspondence is concluded, but no longer than 1 year after the last exchange, after which they are deleted.

4. Who has access to the data? (processors)

  • Web3Forms — forwards form submissions to my email inbox (privacy policy)
  • Google (Gmail) — messages arrive in my email inbox (privacy policy)
  • GitHub Inc. (GitHub Pages) — hosts this site; GitHub may log visitor IP addresses for security purposes (privacy statement)
  • GoatCounter — cookie-free, anonymous visitor statistics; it stores no personal data and cannot identify individual visitors (privacy policy)

Web3Forms, Google and GitHub are US companies; data transfers take place under the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework.

5. Cookies

This site does not use cookies. Visits are measured with GoatCounter, a cookie-free, anonymous statistics tool that collects no personal data — so no cookie consent is required.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, rectification or erasure of your personal data, restriction of processing, object to processing, and exercise your right to data portability. You may withdraw your consent at any time — this does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal. Simply email me at the address above.

7. Remedies

If you believe your data is being processed unlawfully, you may lodge a complaint with the Hungarian National Authority for Data Protection and Freedom of Information (NAIH — Falk Miksa utca 9–11, 1055 Budapest, Hungary; naih.hu; ugyfelszolgalat@naih.hu) or bring the matter before a court.
